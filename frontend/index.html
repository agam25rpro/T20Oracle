Stats Analyst

Fires direct SQL queries against our database of 4,900+ matches. It strips away the noise to find out historically who dominates head-to-head and who leverages the toss best.

Venue Expert

Not all pitches are equal. This agent calculates the average competitive score and mathematically determines if the venue heavily favors chasing or putting runs on the board first.

Player Form Agent

The bridge between history and today. It pulls the top 5 performing batters and bowlers from the DB, then makes a live network call to cricketdata.org (CricAPI) to fetch very recent match scorecards, identifying players currently peaking.

Chief Strategist (Boss)

The final decision maker. Orchestrated via LangGraph, it waits for all three analysts to submit their reports. It weighs historical dominance against live player form to return a structured JSON prediction.
